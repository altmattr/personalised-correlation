Personalised Visualisation of Correlation Plugin for Qualtrics

This site provides a 3D visualisation of the current state of any qualtrics survey. It is optmised for surveys collecting correlated data on a simple scale such as a DSM questionnaire on a five-point scale.

As this plugin is designed to go at the end of a qualtrics survey, the best demonstration is provided by answering a quick demonstration survey. If you would like to see a demonstration visualations, you can see one here.
